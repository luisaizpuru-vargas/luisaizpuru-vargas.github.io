After completing my PhD, I plan to join the private sector to drive innovation in deep tech, focusing on artificial intelligence and its powerful synergies with biology, physics, engineering, and high-performance computing. The ubiquity of artificial intelligence will have profound implications for areas such as transportation, energy, healthcare and science. We will see an exponential growth in the demand for cloud services as well as on the training and inference of A.I. models. From AI-augmented biological design and physics-informed neural networks to autonomous intelligent systems, these converging technologies will revolutionize our economies and societies. With the AI market expected to approach $1.8 trillion by 2030, the potential to engineer novel solutions for humanity’s greatest challenges has never been greater. The future looks bright.

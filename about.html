In my free time I enjoy spending time with family and having long conversations with the many international friends I have made throughout the years. I also enjoy moderate calisthenics.

I am fascinated by the role that vitamin D3 and the amino acid glycine has on human health. Based on my own personal research, I believe that these two molecules hold the key for cellular and epigenetic anti-aging and the reduction of systemic inflammation. Over the past 3 years, I have developed a keen interest on the topic of human reproduction. In a parallel universe, instead of studying physics, I would have become a fertility doctor.

A personal hobby of mine is history. In particular, I am interested in 20th century history. The two topics I am most fascinated by are:

  • The ideological struggles between fascism, communism and liberal democracy.

  • The origins and nature of the Cold War.
